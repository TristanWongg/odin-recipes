Odin's Leg Schlapping Lasagna Recipe

A cheesy and hearty lasagna recipe for the whole family to enjoy!

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe for beginner cooks to try out and enjoy! Preparation time should be no more than an hour. Once prepped, just toss it in the oven and begin salivating while you wait!

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan and/or Mozzarella cheese
  • Various Seasonings: garlic, oregano, basil, italian, salt, pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground meat of preference
  • Vegetables of preference

  1. Boil water and cook the lasagna sheets to al dente
  2. Lightly season and cook the ground meat in a pot
  3. Pour tomato sauce (and any veggies) into the cooked meat and season liberally with all the seasonings to your liking and bring to a simmer
  4. In a lasagna tray, begin making the layers of the lasagna: lasagna sheets, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese (and repeat)
  5. One final layer is completed, add the mozzarella and parmesan cheese on the top generously.
  6. Finally, set oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and bake for 20-25 mins.
