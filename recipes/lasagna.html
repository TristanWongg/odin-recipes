Odin's Leg Schlapping Lasagna Recipe

A cheesy and hearty lasagna recipe for the whole family to enjoy!

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe for beginner cooks to try out and enjoy! Preparation time should be no more than an hour. Once prepped, just toss it in the oven and begin salivating while you wait!

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan and/or Mozzarella cheese
  • Various Seasonings: garlic, oregano, basil, italian, salt, pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground meat of preference
  • Vegetables of preference
