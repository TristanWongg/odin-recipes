Odin's Yummy Ahi Tuna Poke Recipe

Delicious blocks of ahi tuna poke! MMMMMMMMM

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe for beginner cooks to try out and enjoy! No cooking involved since the sushi grade ahi tuna is eaten raw!

  • Ahi tuna steak
  • Japanese light soy sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Minced garlic and ginger
  • Green onions and sweet onions
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Optional: any hot peppers of your liking if you like a kick!

  1. Dice up ahi tuna steak into medium size blocks
  2. Dice up all the onions and peppers
  3. In a mixing bowl, add all the dry ingredients in
  4. Add soy sauce to taste, and a drizzle of sesame oil
  5. Mix everything thoroughly, saran wrap and let it marinate in the fridge for at least 30 mins
  6. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top when ready to serve and enjoy! (typically eaten with white rice)
