Odin's Creamy Avocado Smoothie Recipe

A healthy (kind of) and super creamy avocado shake!

This is a simple and easy to follow recipe for a refreshing beverage!

  • Avocado (1 Large)
  • Condensed milk (1/2 cup)
  • Ice (1 cup)
  • Milk (1/2 cup)

  1. Cut and peel the avocado
  2. In a blender, add all the ingredients and blend (ratios can be adjusted to your preference)
  3. Enjoy!
